Mariana's cake Recipe

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake Recipe. Enojy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 25 min

Ingredients

Black Chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80 g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the buteer together

2

Mix the sugar, egs and the flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
